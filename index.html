Welcome to ASECNA

For over 60 years, ASECNA has been the cornerstone of air navigation safety and coordination for its 19 Member States spanning West Africa, Central Africa, and the Indian Ocean. We are committed to providing world-class air navigation services, ensuring the safety and efficiency of flights within one of the largest airspace blocks in the world.
